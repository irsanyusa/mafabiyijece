Did you know men and women see colours differently?

05 Jul 2024, 00:00

This topic is fascinating because it reveals how our brains work and how our biology affects our everyday experiences. The science behind colour vision Our eyes have special cells called cones that help us see colours. There are three types of cones, each sensitive to red, green, or blue light. The way these cones respond to light can vary between men and women. Studies have shown that women have more cones, particularly those sensitive to red and green.
