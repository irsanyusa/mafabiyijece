    Body Image has been an integral part of Meghan Trainor’s career, but the singer recently decided to take the next step. The main motivation behind her weight loss journey is her son who she gave birth to via C-section. The singer recently revealed that she was in a “dark place,” after reaching her heaviest weight ever while carrying her son.

    Meghan Trainor Lost Over 60 Pounds

    Months after becoming a mother to her now 1-year-old son Riley Sabara, Trainor revealed she struggled with her body image. At the time, the Grammy Award winner explained that she had gained the most weight in her life after welcoming her first child with her husband Daryl Sabara.

    “I was my heaviest I’d ever been, I was 200 pounds. I just wasn’t feeling great.” Trainor said. “To have this C-section scar, I was in a really dark place, and I wanted to be in a great place for my son.”

    Trainor revealed that her fitness journey stemmed from her desire to be a great role model for her son Riley. Since then, Trainor has changed her fitness routine to everyday workouts and transitioned to a healthier diet.

    “So I worked [out] every day and challenged myself. And I was like, ‘If I can survive a C-section, I can do anything.'” Trainor said. “I learned that I do like healthy food, and I learned what portions mean.”

    The Clash of the Cover Bands judge revealed she’s at the best place of her life and plans to stay that way. Keeping her as the main motivation will also help Trainor out in staying healthy in the long run.

    She Opened Up About Dropping Takin It Back on the Same Day as Taylor Swift’s Midnights

    Trainor released her fourth studio album Takin’ it Back on October 21, the same day Taylor Swift dropped Midnights. Contrary to the competition, Trainor revealed that she feels a special kind of kinship to her fellow artist.

    “It was a big day and just a good day for pop and successful women,” Trainor said. “It’s a lot of support and love out there. Which is beautiful because they’re not pinning us against each other or trying to make it a competing thing.”

    Another superstar who released her latest album is Carly Rae Jepsen with The Loneliest Time. All three albums were able to make it to the top 20 of the Billboard 200 albums chart. Trainor expressed excitement for pop music welcoming three impressive new albums from well-known singers.
