    America’s Got Talent: All-Stars has aired five episodes so far, and we still have a few contestants left to see. The schedule for upcoming episodes of the show includes one more performance episode before a pre-finale special leading into the Finals.

    AGT: All-Stars Schedule Includes Pre-Finale Special

    On Monday night, the sixth episode of All-Stars will air, with 10 more acts taking the stage for the chance to go to the Finals. All five Golden Buzzers have been given out, with 10 acts in the Finals so far. Since the Finals will only feature 11 acts, that means only one of Monday’s performers will make it through, based on the Superfan vote.

    You might expect the next episode after that to be the Finals. However, NBC will instead be airing a special in the show’s usual timeslot called Finals Preview: From the Judges’ Desk on Monday, February 13. So fans will have to wait a little longer to see the final performances from their favorite acts.

    It looks like the Finals will finally air the next week, on February 20. The finalists will perform for the Superfans’ votes, and the winner will be announced in what NBC has described as a “two-hour star-studded finale,” which seems to be scheduled for February 27.

    Which Acts Made It Through to the Finals?

    Interestingly, not a single AGT winner has made it through to the Finals so far, although two of them (Terry Fator and Dustin Tavella) have performed. Instead, several other fan-favorite AGT acts, as well as a few acts from around the world, have been named finalists.

    The judges and host chose five Golden Buzzer acts to go to the Finals. They include Light Balance Kids (Howie Mandel), Detroit Youth Choir (Terry Crews) Mike E. Winfield (Simon Cowell), Aidan McCann (Heidi Klum), and Tom Ball (Group Golden Buzzer).

    The Superfans also voted five more acts through to the Finals: Bello Sisters, Aidan Bryant, Avery Dixon, Power Duo, and Ana-Maria Mărgean. Of the 10 acts voted through so far, four of them (Aidan McCann, Tom Ball, Power Duo, and Ana-Maria Mărgean) have never appeared on AGT before.

    Two more AGT winners are performing in Monday’s show: Season 14 winner Kodi Lee and Season 15 winner Brandon Leake. Will one of them take the last spot in the Finals? We’ll have to wait and see when the show airs at 8/7c on NBC.
