    These questions that you often hear people ask. Pulse Religion is here to offer all the answers you need. Here are three great ways to live a Godly life.

    Have faith

    Hebrews 11: 6 says, “ And without faith, it is impossible to please God because anyone who approaches Him must believe that He exists and that He rewards those who earnestly seek Him.”

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Having faith is the first thing you need to do as a Christian. Without this, it is impossible to please God, maintain a great relationship with Him or live a life that pleases Him.

    Read/ Study the Bible on a daily basis

    Your faith is built by the scriptures as seen in Romans 10:17, which says, “Consequently, faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of Christ.”

    Your faith is strengthened by the scriptures, on the other hand, your path is made straight by God’s Words and you get everything you need to have a Godly life.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Psalms 119:105— “Thy word is a lamp unto my feet and a light unto my path.”

    Prayer is the key

    No wonder 1 Thessalonians 5:16–18 says, “Rejoice always, pray without ceasing, give thanks in all circumstances; for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus for you.”

    We need to tell God about everything from our issues to our blessings. Doing this helps us maintain a Godly lifestyle.
