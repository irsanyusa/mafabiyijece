    Arthur Gunn released the video for his song “Nyano Ghar.” The American Idol alum decided to create a video from the song because of the strong reaction from his fans from the lyric video. The singer was a standout during Season 18 of Idol as well as Season 19.

    “‘Nyano Ghar’ is the song that I wrote many years ago for the album Grahan, now it is more like a long lost friend. After the massive views on the lyrics video of this song, I decided to do the rendition of the song and make the video, this is what we came up with!” Arthur stated on YouTube.

    Arthur sings the song in his native Nepali language. The song, which means house of warmth, has been one of his more popular singles from his 2018 album. The lyrics paint a picture of Arthur being a helpless romantic and wanting his love back. The official music video has already amassed close to 15,000 views. The black and white video shows Arthur with his guitar in hand.

    Arthur was 21 years old when he walked in to audition for Season 18 of American Idol. The judges were smitten with the young singer. The uniqueness of his voice stood out. The singer made it through to Hollywood and dominated the competition. Ultimately he finished the season as the runner-up to Just Sam.

    Arthur Gunn’s “Nyano Ghar” Video Drops Ahead of Season 20 Premiere

    Arthur was one of the more popular American Idol contestants in recent years. When it was time for Season 19’s newly implemented Comeback Round, there was no doubt that he would enter that season’s Top 10. The singer did not have much luck during his return, he was eliminated in the Top 7.

    The singer was invited to sing alongside Sheryl Crow for the Season 19 finale. Arthur did not show. He stated that he missed the show because of an unpleasant environment. He later went on to thank American Idol for the experience.

    Fans can catch up with Arthur on his Instagram. The singer continues to show off his love for photography. He also continues to share new music that he writes.

    Arthur isn’t the first Idol alum who is releasing a new project close to the premiere of the show’s 20th season. Hunter Metts also announced new music dropping in the coming weeks.
