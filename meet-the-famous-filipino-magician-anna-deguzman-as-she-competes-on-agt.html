    The world of magic is about to witness an extraordinary talent as Anna DeGuzman, a renowned magician, cardist, and mentalist, takes the stage on the widely popular reality show America’s Got Talent season 18. Hailing from Bergenfield, New Jersey, Anna has captivated audiences from all walks of life with her enchanting performances and unmatched skill.

    Who is Anna DeGuzman?

    Born on August 21, 1998, Anna is a Leo, known for her passion, charisma, and natural showmanship. Growing up as an only child, she discovered her love for magic and cardistry at an early age.

    At just 16 years old, she made waves in the magic community by becoming the only female registrant at Cardistry-Con, a prestigious gathering of cardistry enthusiasts. Her dedication and talent also caught the attention of the Sacramento Kings. They then invited her to their media day to amaze their players with her mind-blowing tricks.

    Anna proudly embraces her Filipino heritage, representing the rich diversity of the magic community. Through her performances, she has become an inspiration for aspiring magicians worldwide. Showcasing the power of pursuing one’s passion and then turning dreams into reality.

    With expertise in entertainment and branding, Anna has established herself as one of the most influential magicians in the world. Her incredible dexterity and mastery of cardistry have been showcased on various prominent platforms, including Disney, ESPN, MTV, and also CW.

    She Has Performed On Many Different Platforms

    The versatility of Anna’s talents has allowed her to perform in diverse settings, ranging from impromptu street shows to elite invitation-only cocktail parties and also high-profile private events. Notably, she has even graced the prestigious stage of Australia’s Got Talent in 2022.

    Anna’s magical journey has been filled with numerous highlights and achievements. Popular television shows like The Steve Harvey Show, MTV’s Amazingness, and CW’s Penn & Teller: Fool Us have also recognized her exceptional talents. Her exceptional abilities have also attracted renowned companies like Microsoft, Playboy, and Christian Louboutin. They sought her enchanting performances to captivate their audiences.

    As she competes on AGT, Anna aims to bring her unique brand of magic to a broader audience. Her extraordinary talent, combined with her magnetic charm, promises to captivate both the judges and viewers alike.
