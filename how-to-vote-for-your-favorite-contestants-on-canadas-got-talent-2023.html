    Canada’s Got Talent season two is looming its premiere on Citytv. At this point, fans can only expect to witness the best acts and identify the potential winner as early as possible. Here’s how you can support your favorite acts.

    How To Vote For Your Favorite Act in Canada’s Got Talent?

    In competition series like Got Talent, judges are not the only people that can change the lives of the show’s contestants. The crowd itself is powerful enough to let someone be champion. Thus, the need to be informed about the contest’s voting rules.

    There are two voting options that voters can choose from to save their desired contestant. Practically, either a website vote or a Smartphone vote will suffice.

    As per the website voting option, fans can open their browser to cast their ballot for their favorite acts. They simply have to open the official Citytv.com website, create an account, and join in. A “Vote Now” banner for Canada’s Got Talent will appear. Fans shall then select the button and choose the candidates whom they want to cast their vote for. A “VOTE” button is visible on screen, and fans can just click the same to record their votes.

    Per voting window, fans may cast votes for their desired acts up to three times. Nonetheless, this time, there will be no call, phone, text, and social media voting options. The voting period for each episode lasts for 24 hours.

    CGT Set to Air on March 21, 2023

    Successfully finishing two seasons in the past years, Canada’s Got Talent has been renewed for another one. The much-anticipated third season of the series is finally coming a few hours away. On March 21, 2023, Canada is once again lucky to witness a new set of talented acts each week.

    In a recent Instagram post, the series’ account has asked its fans to unplug their phone, lock the doors, and grab popcorn for the family. Subsequently, fans are already excited for the show.

    For months, Canada’s Got Talent has been teasing fans with sneak peek videos of judges’ reactions to performances and thrilling invitations from them. There were also a few contestant introductions posted. This week though, fans can wait no more as the biggest talent competition in Canada premieres this Tuesday, 8/7c on Citytv.
