    The Voice Season 21 artist Jeremy Rosado has released his first new single since signing a record deal with Capitol Christian Music Group earlier this year. The soulful worship song is called “Believe You Will.”

    Jeremy Rosado Releases Song ‘Believe You Will’

    “Believe You Will” shows off Rosado’s powerful and emotional voice, as he sings, “You will / Turn this into something good / Show up right on time for me / Do everything you said you would / I believe I believe I believe you will.”

    “This song means so much to me…” Rosado wrote on Instagram earlier this month when teasing the track for fans. “I have lived these words, I’ve experienced God showing up right on time, so many times!”

    In August, Rosado announced that he had signed a record deal with Capital Music/Capitol Christian Music Group, calling it a “dream come true.” He shared that “all of my favorite Christian artists are signed to this label.”

    Rosado Is Currently on Tour with Newsboys

    This month, Rosado is on tour with the Christian rock band Newsboys. He’s a special guest on the tour, along with Cochren & Co. and Adam Agee. The singer has been performing “Believe You Will” during the recent concerts, and now fans can finally stream it.

    Fans expressed their love for the song on Instagram, with one commenter writing, “Already have it in a playlist.” Another simply wrote, “YES ITS SO GOOD.” They’re no doubt ready to hear more from the singer as he continues his musical journey.

    Rosado competed on Season 21 of The Voice on Kelly Clarkson’s team, making it to the Top 10. Before the show, he released the 2015 album Heartbeat, followed by several more singles and EPs. He previously competed on American Idol in Season 11, making it to 13th place.
