    America’s Got Talent Season 17 finalist Drake Milligan is set to take the stage during Wednesday night’s results show, as we find out which two acts from Tuesday’s show made it through to the Finals thanks to America’s vote.

    Drake Milligan to Perform During AGT Results Show

    On Tuesday, Milligan took to Instagram to share a photo of himself with AGT Season 18 contestant and fellow country singer Kylie Frey, who performed on this week’s live show. Milligan encouraged everyone to “show her some love.”

    “Feels good to be back in my @agt stomping grounds!” Milligan wrote of being back on the show’s set. “I’ll be back on @agt playing my new song #IGotAProblem on Wednesday night, so tune in and I’ll see ya there.”

    Milligan auditioned for AGT Season 17 with his band. The country singer charmed everyone with his original songs. He ended up making it all the way to the Finals, where he placed third in the competition.

    Following his time on the show, Milligan released his debut album, Dallas/Forth Worth. Recently, the singer released a new single called “I Got a Problem.”

    Which Acts Will Go Through to the Finale?

    On Tuesday night, 11 more acts took the AGT stage for a chance to earn America’s vote and go through to the Finals. Those acts were Anna DeGuzman, Chibi Unity, D’Corey Johnson, Freedom Singers, Kylie Frey, Mariandrea, Orlando Leyba, Puppet Simon and The Cow Belles, Roland Abante, Shadow Ace, and Zion Clark.

    As of this writing, singer Roland Abante has the most views for his latest performance on YouTube, at over a million. The rest of the acts don’t even come close to that. Even Golden Buzzer act Chibi Unity has less than 200,000 views for their new performance.

    Is this a sign that Roland will make it through to the Finals? We won’t know for sure until the results show. Be sure to watch Wednesday night on NBC to find out if your favorites were voted through.
