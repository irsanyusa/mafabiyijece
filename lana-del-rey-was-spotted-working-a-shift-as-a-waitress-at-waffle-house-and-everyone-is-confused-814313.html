    28 May 2024, 00:00

    Fans were surprised to see Lana Del Rey in a restaurant, but this time, not as a customer. The singer was spotted at a Waffle House working a shift. No one understands why the star decided to change her career path. However, we have some believable guesses.

    Lana was working in Florence.

    The singer was working a shift in Florence, Alabama. The customers were lucky to meet her on Thursday on July 20 behind the Waffle House counter. She was wearing a blue uniform and a name tag “Lana”. Fans captured the moment when she was brewing coffee.

    She took photos with her fans.

    Lana was also happy to pose for pictures. A fan Karina Cisneros Juarez told about her encounter with Lana Del Rey, “It was a bit surreal. I just told her how much I loved her music and her work in general. She was super lovely and incredibly nice.”

    No one knows why Lana was there.

    Once the photos circulated on the Internet, everyone got confused. Some said that she was “exploring a new career path”. Others believed that Lana was trying to record a music video, or it was just a publicity stunt for her upcoming songs.

    Lana isn’t working on any project at the moment.

    Interestingly, local recording venues FAME Studios and Muscle Shoals Sound mentioned that she hadn’t been recording anything with them. Neither Lana nor Waffle House responded as to why she was working there.
