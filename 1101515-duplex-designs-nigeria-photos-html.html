    When we say "duplex" we mean a detached comfortable cottage, suitable for two apartments with separate entrances. It's like a rule. However, there are exceptions to any rule. Just check out these unconventional and fascinating duplex designs in Nigeria.

    In different countries, even in different regions of the same country, this term can imply something a little bit special. Overall it continues to stay within the concept of "comfortable low-rise cottage". Duplex is a combination of a cottage and an apartment. It combines the advantages of a country mansion and the comfort of urban housing.

    A bit of history. Where did duplex houses come from?

    In the 19th century in England, keeping with traditions, big families lived in spacious mansions. Their children grew up. It is natural that they began to think about their own family home. A big family mansion has been already built, but it also had its limitations. What did they manage to invent?

    Well, the children decided to build a mansion with the separate entrance next to the side wall of the family home. Two related families continued to live in the same house, divided into two autonomous sections. The word 'duplex' itself is derived from this concept. In Latin, it means "composed of two parts."

    Duplex is convenient, especially for a big family: one-half can be for the younger family and the other part for the parents. On the one hand, it prevents possible conflicts of generations and on the other â€“ extended family will live almost together, ready to help at the first call.

    Latest duplex designs in Nigeria

    Nigeria, as a former British colony, has adopted the custom of building duplex homes. Modern duplex house designs in Nigeria have some features in common. Duplex is usually the best choice if the neighbors you are going to choose are parents, relatives or maybe even close friends.

    Each apartment has its own porch, own driveway as well as an adjacent side wall garage and land. This land can be used as a playground or a lawn. It can also serve as a garden where you can plant beautiful flowers.

    The layout of the duplex

    In some cases, such houses can be configured with one entrance hall (which is the only one for both families). This house planning will be ideal for an extended family who shares one house and likes to spend a lot of time together. The general practice is that the ground floor contains a kitchen and a dining room which can be combined with the living room.

    On the second floor, there are two separate sections with a bathroom and several bedrooms in each one. It is considered to be a great combination of rooms' arrangement in order to preserve the personal space of each family member.

    Basically, the layout of this type of home should be always functional and comfortable. In each independent half of the house, it is necessary to provide what is most urgently needed. In addition, the desire of the owner may be added the layout, for instance, gym or game room.

    Exterior design of duplex

    The external design of a duplex house is subject to individual preferences of the owners. The outside of the houses must have a uniform composition. The exterior design of duplex houses should be mutually agreed to by those living in it. Everything depends mainly on the coordination between neighbors and their personal preferences and also the financial capabilities of the owner of each duplex.

    It is your personal decision whether to live in a separate house or to share a duplex with somebody. The most important thing is to organize and design your own home to your taste and to create good vibes within your family.
